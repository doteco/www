About the .eco system

We know you’re proud of the work you do to protect the environment — whether it’s making green products, conserving the world’s wild spaces or living an eco-friendly lifestyle.
The .eco system showcases your environmental actions and commitments to millions of people around the world who share your values.

When you buy a .eco domain name, you get:

A .eco domain name of your choice

A .eco profile for showcasing environmental actions

A .eco trustmark, similar to a Facebook or Twitter icon, that links to your .eco profile affirms you are a legitimate .eco community member.
