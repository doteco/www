Trevor Bowden

Trevor is co-founder of the .eco registry. Before .eco, Trevor was a consultant with Sustainable Finance, a UK consultancy that advised the worldâ€™s largest banks on how to manage environmental and social risks in project finance activities. Trevor also worked at the United Nations Environment Programme Finance Initiative where he met .eco co-founder, Jacob Malthouse.

Jacob Malthouse

Co-founder of Big Room, the .eco registry

jacob@home.eco

Before co-founding the .eco registry, Jacob was Liaison to the Caribbean and Canada for the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN), the international body that governs Internet address. At ICANN, he also led development of a Fellowships Programme that brought hundreds of Internet leaders from developing countries to engage in international Internet policy making.

Prior to ICANN, Jacob led the Investment Team at the UN Environment Programme's Finance Initiative (UNEP FI), where he was responsible for co-founding the UN Principles for Responsible Investment. In 2000, he was selected as a Young Canadian Leader for a Sustainable Future by the International Institute for Sustainable Development. Jacob holds a BA in Geography from the University of Victoria.
