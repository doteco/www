.eco is the must-have extension for anyone commited to positive change for the planet.

For the environment — why .eco?

The general public will be looking for the .eco extension as it gives companies and organisations credibility as it fast becomes the global standard to look for. As a term, "eco" is already:

Specific: "eco" is defined by the Oxford English Dictionary with a single definition: "not harming the environment"

Applicable: "eco" naturally affixes itself to any noun or verb to generate meaning. It can be used with products, companies, organizations, individuals, or actions.

Recognized: Perhaps as importantly, "eco" is recognized as an environmental term in most languages, including in Latin America and Asia.

With .eco, your customers will stand out from the crowd. They will own the only domain extension that is backed by the environmental community. It is the new symbol that will be used to denote a true commitment to sustainability.

  • A 2014 Shelton Group study showed that 70% of US consumers claimed to be consciously searching for greener products.
  • A 2013 Shelton Group study showed that 67% of business decision makers say sustainability is an important factor when they make purchasing decisions.
  • A 2012 Vision Critical Survey showed that 64% of US small business owners expect owners of .eco domain names to have made a specific commitment to the environment.
