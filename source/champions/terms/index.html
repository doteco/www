PROGRAM TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Big Room Inc (“Big Room”) is the registry operator for the .eco top level domain (“.eco TLD”). This Program is an initiative designed to engage early adopters, also known as “.eco champions,” to proactively develop and maintain domains with the .eco top level domain prior to the public launch in April 2017 (the “Program”). All aspects of the Program are governed by the Terms and Conditions (as defined below).

1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 “.eco Domain”
means a domain name in the .eco top level domain.
1.2 “.eco Champion”
means an Applicant whose Application has been approved by Big Room for the Program, who has executed the Champions Agreement, and who is in good standing under the Program.
1.3 “.eco Champion Website”
means a website developed by or on behalf of a .eco Champion using the Approved Domain that was the subject of such .eco Champion’s Application.
1.4 “Applicant”
means a party that submits an Application for a .eco Domain under the Program.
1.5 “Application”
means the information, answers and proposals contained in the Applicant’s request for a .eco Domain name under the Program submitted to Big Room, as well as any subsequent information, correspondence or supplemental materials submitted to Big Room in connection therewith for the purpose of being approved by Big Room for participation in the Program.
1.6 “Application Process”
means the entire application and approval process for domain names under the Program, including, without limitation, the drafting and submission of Applications by Applicants (including any supplemental materials that may be requested by Big Room), any communication between an Applicant and Big Room relating to the Program, Big Room’s review, consideration, approval and rejection of Applications, and the execution of the Champions Agreement.
1.7 “Approved Domain”
means a .eco Domain that is the subject of an Application which has been approved by Big Room for participation in the Program.
1.8 “Champion Agreement”
means the contract provided by Big Room to approved Applicants governing their use of an Approved Domain.
1.9 “Proposed Plan”
means an Applicant’s proposed development, usage and marketing plan for a .eco Domain as described in their Application and in any subsequent materials and correspondence provided to Big Room in connection therewith.
1.10 “Registrar”
means any entity accredited by Big Room as a registrar for .eco Domains.
1.11 “Registration”
means the registration in the name of a .eco Champion of an Approved Domain.
1.12 “Terms and Conditions”
means the complete terms and conditions applicable to the Program, including those set forth in these .eco Champions Program Terms and Conditions, in the Champions Agreement, communicated to the Applicant by Big Room during the Application Process, all of which may be modified by Big Room from time to time in its sole discretion.

2. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The following is a brief summary of the various steps in the Program ultimately leading to Registration of an Approved Domain:

  1. (a) Application Submission: Applicants submit completed Applications in accordance with these Terms and Conditions by April 17, 2017. By submitting an Application, Applicants agree to comply with and be bound by the Terms and Conditions.
  2. (b) Application Review: Big Room reviews Applications for compliance with Program criteria and assesses the extent to which the Proposed Plan would contribute to the global environmental community in a meaningful way.
  3. (c) Approval: Big Room approves those Applications it determines are best suited for the Program.
  4. (d) Champion Agreement Execution: Approved Applicants execute the Champion Agreement and become .eco Champions, which entitles them to license their Approved Domains from Big Room for the Licence Term, as agreed in the Champion Agreement.
  5. (e) Registration: .eco Champions that have completed the Program in good standing are given the opportunity to continue to renew Approved Domains through a Registrar of their choice (standard registration fees apply).
  6. (f) Site Development, Launch and Operation: Unless otherwise mutually agreed upon by Big Room and the applicable .eco Champion, .eco Champions shall develop and launch no later than April 18, 2017, and operate their .eco Champion Websites in accordance with the Terms and Conditions.
