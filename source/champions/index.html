Become a .eco champion

We are excited to offer you - as a global sustainability leader - assured access to .eco. You will have the option to use this domain before .eco domains are made available to the public. Your championing of it will set the standard for those that follow.

By being named as a .eco Champion you agree to:

  • Begin actively using this domain in 2017
  • Agree a plan with us to jointly promote your .eco website
