Who is .eco for

Whether it's a campaign to protect a special place in the world,

the launch of a new business or product that will help people make better choices, or

a new address for an existing online presence,

.eco will help organizations, businesses and people get recognition for the good work that they do.

A symbol for those that do good

.eco is for organizations, businesses and people committed to positive change for the planet. If you (or your client) has a registered trademark in the Trademark Clearing House, you can participate in Sunrise beginning on February 1, 2017.

Registering and activating a .eco domain is easy. Businesses, organizations and people can qualify in a number of ways. Find out more about the .eco eligibility requirements

If you’re not a trademark holder, register your email address to be kept up to date, as .eco domains will be available to other businesses, organizations and people starting from April 18, 2017.
