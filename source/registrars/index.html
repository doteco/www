A domain for change

.eco is the must-have extension for anyone committed to positive change for the planet.

People want to be eco-friendly

With .eco, your customers be recognized for the good work they do. A recent study by Nielsen research found that over half of global online consumers in 60 countries say they want products and services that are better for people and the planet.

A new symbol for positive action, .eco is:

Authentic: Created with the support of over 50 environmental organizations from around the world, .eco is the only domain extension back by the environmental community.

Trusted: The .eco System includes a .eco domain name, .eco Profile and .eco Trustmark so your customers will instantly be recognized for the good work they do in support of the planet.

Global: Understood around the world, the word "eco" means environment, so people will know your customer is part of a worldwide movement committed to making positive environmental progress.
