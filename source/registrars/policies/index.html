Registry policies

To activate a .eco domain

Complete the following steps within the .eco domain name's Eco Profile

  1. Affirm the eligibility of the .eco registrant(see below)

  2. Take a simple pledge to show commitment to the purpose and principles of .eco

These minimum requirements apply to all .eco domain names, including those registered in the Sunrise period.

Eligibility

.eco is open to organizations, businesses and people committed to positive change for the planet

If you're wondering who qualifies fpr a .eco domain, we want you to know the .eco community is for any registrant who commits to a respectful, responsible, and sustainable use of the environment

.eco Registrants can show the world their commitment in one of two ways:

  • by accepting a free membership in the Dot ECO Global Community Organization when completing the registration for a .eco domain, OR
  • by meeting any of the criteria below:
    1. Not-for-profit mission-based organizations, both small and large, that work for the good of the planet.

      2. Not-for-profit environmental organizations that can provide proof of their environmental mission/purpose and their legal status or accreditation as such by United Nations agencies

    2. Businesses of all sizes committed to providing products and services that encourage positive environmental change.
    3. Government bodies that support sustainability through programs, infrastructure and funding amongst other policies and actions.
    4. People who participate in and support environmental change.
    5. Farms and other producers of agricultural products that achieve EU organic production and labelling standards.
