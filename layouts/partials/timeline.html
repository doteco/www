.eco Launch Schedule

The launch schedule has the following phases and dates. Each phase starts at 16:00 UTC.

Sunrise

Feb 1 – Apr 2, 2017

Trademark Clearinghouse (TMCH) Sunrise for rights holders that have registered their trademark.

Early Access

April 18 – 25, 2017 (Earth Week)

Advanced registration open to everyone via a first-come, first-served, 7-day early access program.

General Availability

April 25, 2017

Domains will be available on a first-come, first-serve basis.
