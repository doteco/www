Funding your Account

There are two ways to fund your account:

  • Pre-payment accounts
    If you are using pre-payment, please ensure your account has sufficient funds by sending funds to Big Room, the .eco Registry using the instructions below. Once received, we will update your account accordingly.
  • Post-payment accounts
    If you have already agreed to a post-payment option, we will update your account accordingly. Payments should be made using the information below. If you would like to request a post-payment account, please email Big Room, the .eco Registry at finance@bigroom.ca
